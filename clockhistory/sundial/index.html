What is sundial?

A sundial is a device that tells the time of day when there is sunlight by the apparent position of the Sun  in the sky. 

How does sundial work?

From the earliest times people have regulated their lives by the apparent motions of the Sun and the shadows cast by its rays. We say ‘apparent’ because of course it is the rotation of the Earth on its axis which causes the moving shadows we observe every day. When the Earth rotates 15° it is just as though the Sun had moved 15° around its daily track

What problem did sundial solve?

Can let people know the exact time.

What problem does sundial has?

If the weather is cloudy, we won't know the time.
