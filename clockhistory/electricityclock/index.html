What is Electricity Clock?

An electricity clock is a clock that is powered by electricity, as opposed to a mechanical clock which is powered by a hanging weight or a  mainspring.

How Electricity Clock work?

A  electricity clock does not contain a timekeeping oscillator such as a pendulum or balance wheel, but instead counts the oscillations of the AC utility current from its wall plug to keep time. It consists of a small AC synchronous motor, which turns the clock's hands through a reduction gear train.
